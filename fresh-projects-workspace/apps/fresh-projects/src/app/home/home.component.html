Fresh Projects Floorplan App

What this app does

Explore a property’s floorplan and interact with its rooms. Selecting a room reveals its details and a dedicated gallery of images. A main gallery is also available to browse all floorplan images.

Layout & Behaviour

Floorplan Panel

  • Shows the floorplan image with clickable “hotspots” for each room.
  • On hover, rooms are highlighted; on click, that room becomes selected.

Room Details Panel

  • Displays the selected room’s Name and Description.
  • Includes a Room Gallery filtered to that room’s images.
  • Empty state shown until a room is selected (prompting you to click a room).

Main Gallery

  • Appears below the Room Details panel.
  • Shows the broader collection of images for the entire floorplan.
  • Optimized for small screens with swipe/arrow navigation.

How to use

  1. Hover over the floorplan to locate a room; click the room to select it.
  2. Review the room’s details and browse its Room Gallery.
  3. Scroll to the Main Gallery to explore all images of the floorplan.
